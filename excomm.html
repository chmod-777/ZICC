Executive Committe

Phibion Tachiona Makoni - Chairman

Mr Makoni is a well-known businessman and is the Chairman of the SGI Group in Zimbabwe and its regional subsidiaries, with business experience in various sectors ranging from transportation to mining, Mr Makoni brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to ZICC. Mr. Makoni also serves/ has served as Chairman of a number of Mining Companies in Zimbabwe and Zambia. He also serves as:

  1. Chairman of FIDI Africa, an Africa-wide Board for International Moving Companies,
  2. Representative of BEML in Zimbabwe
  3. Director of Logistics Services companies in Mozambique, Zambia and Malawi.
Mr Pradeep Varyani - Vice-Chairman

Mr Varyani, an Indian national, in Zimbabwe since 1998, holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree. Mr Varyani has business interests in the paper and packaging industry in Africa since last 30 years. He is a director in the Vista Group of Companies and has his offices in 7 countries including the Middle East, India, China and Singapore. Mr. Varyani brings a strong knowledge base of doing international business and has been a member of various international trade and social organisations. He is a Trustee of ‘The Art of Living’Foundation.

Rosetta Elizabeth Peters - Secretary

Ms Peters, a successful business woman, is a Board Member of various Institutions. Ms. Peters is also a member of Rotary International. She was the first woman president of the Mashonaland Chamber of Industries / Confederation of Zimbabwe Industries (CZI) overseeing over 21 sectors of Industry in Zimbabwe at the time.

Ashok Chakravarti - Treasurer

Mr Chakravarti holds a M.A., M. Phil. (Economics), Oxford University, U.K. and a B.A. Hons. (Economics), Delhi University, India. Mr Chakravarti is involved in a number of social and business organisations including:

  • Senior Visiting Lecturer, Dept. Of Economics, University of Zimbabwe
  • Consultant, Ministry of Finance, Government of Zimbabwe (DFID/Crown Agents project)
  • Managing Director, Baines 24 Hour Emergency Rooms, Harare and Mutare
  • Treasurer, Zimbabwe-India Chamber of Commerce
  • Treasurer, Friendly Diplomatic Golfers Association
  • Author, “Institutions, Economic Performance and the Visible Hand”, Edward Elgar, U.K.
  • Author, “Aid, Institutions and Development”, Edward Elgar, U.K.
Sanjay Babbar

Mr Babbar is the Managing Director and Owner AML and Distributors of Mahindra (Vehicles and Agricultural Tractors), Ashok Leyland (Trucks and Buses) and Kia Motors (Vehicles). Mr Babbar holds a MSc Manufacturing Engineering, BSc Electrical Engineering. Mr Babbar is Committee member of: Zimbabwe Agricultural Show (ZAS), CZI Trade and Investment, Harare Mayors Cheer Fund (HMCF) and the Friendly Diplomatic Golfers Association (FDGA). Mr Babbar is a business man with knowledge in many sectors.

Ashwini Saxena

Mr. Ashwini Saxena holds Masters degrees in Geology and Business Management (with specialisation in enterprise & strategic management). He is also trained on Foundations of Project Management by PMI, USA. He has 24 years of diverse experience with government, corporates and international organisations such as UNIDO and IFC (World Bank). He has worked on various projects of rural development, MSME Cluster Development andMSME policies. He has been involved in piloting of the “Financial Valuation Tool for Community Investments” developed by IFC. He serves on a number of development and professional networks and advisory groups. He heads the corporate responsibility activities of the Essar Group in Zimbabwe and the region as General Manager (CSR).

Stephen Margolis

Mr Margolis is owner of Margolis Holdings P/L, involved in Real Estate development and International Trade owns industrial land sub – divided for sale. He owns Margolis Plaza which covers the whole block of land from Harare Street to Kaguvi Street on Speke Avenue. He is a proud owner of the Stephen Margolis Resort and Margolis Trading P/L which trades with Government departments

Nhau George Chitsinde

Mr Chitsinde is a CEO, Founder and projects director of Hagadol Enterprises (PVT) Ltd. which specialises in software development , web hosting , SEO , aimed at connecting Zimbabwe and India to the rest of the world. Assisting other thriving SMEs worldwide to market themselves.20 Years Entrepreneurial experience mainly in the software consultancy and project management field in various corporations.

MUSTAFA SACHAK

Mr. Mustafa is the CEO of TA Insurance which is TA Holdings insurance group operating in Botswana, Uganda and Zimbabwe with investments in general insurance, life assurance, reinsurance, asset management and microfinance. Mr. Mustafa started his career at Motorola in the USA where he spent 10 years in the Communication Division before moving to Zimbabwe in October of 1996 to pursue a different career path. He graduated from University College London with a BSc in Chemical Engineering in 1980 and a BSc in Electrical Engineering from Florida Atlantic University in 1986 and an MBA from Florida International University whilst working for Motorola. He was appointed to the Board on 04 December 2013.
