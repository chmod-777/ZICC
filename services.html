Services

ZICC has a broad range of activities covering various aspects of business & trade issues viz. trade missions; policy matters; assistance to new entrepreneurs; business advice/consultancy; networking within Zimbabwe and India; investment; human resource development; amongst others.

ZICC works closely with the Embassy of India, Harare and the Ministry of Trade, Industry and Commerce, Government of Zimbabwe, to facilitate any required interface and dialogue for its members.

ZICC members get:
  • Opportunities for B2B contacts and linkages with investors, companies and individuals through various events

  • Information and research support to facilitate such B2B linkages

  • General economic research inputs to facilitate business decisions across various sectors

  • Regular updates on various trade and investment events related to the interests of ZICC members
