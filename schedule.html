Date: March 2013 | Mr Anand Sharma, Hon'ble Minister of Commerce Address
During his visit to Zimbabwe in March 2013, Mr Anand Sharma, Hon’ble Minister of Commerce, Industry and Textiles addressed a gathering of members of the ZICC along with Prof. Welshman Ncube, Hon’ble Minister of Industry and Commerce of the Republic of Zimbabwe.

Date: 25th – 26th July, 2013 | Global R&D Summit 2013 | New Delhi
Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FICCI) in partnership with Department of Science & Technology, Government of India, organized a two-day international R&D conference and exhibition titled 'Global R&D Summit 2013 – Destination India' held on 25th – 26th July 2013 at The Ashok, New Delhi, India.

Date: August 2013 |India Food Festival
Embassy of India in Harare in association with ZICC organised an Indian Food Festival in collaboration with Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, as well as an Exhibition of Indian Handicrafts in collaboration with EPCH, India, in August 2013

Date: August 2013 | PubliCon 2013: Export Markets | Delhi
Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI) organised a two-day conference "PubliCon 2013: Export Markets" on 10th – 11th September 2013, at FICCI, Federation House, Tansen Marg, New Delhi.

Dates: 23 – 25 September, 2013 |Global Economic Summit on MSME Clusters | Mumbai
World Trade Centre (WTC) Mumbai, India, and All India Association of Industries (AIAI) are jointly organized the third edition of Global Economic Summit on MSME Clusters from September 23-25, 2013 at World Trade Centre, Mumbai, India. This Summit was supported by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India and Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise (MSME), Government of India.


Dates: 15 to 18th October |2013 – Indian Handicrafts & Gifts Fair (Autumn) 2013 | Delhi
Export Promotion Council for Handicrafts (EPCH) organized the 36th edition of Indian Handicrafts & Gifts Fair (Autumn) 2013 at India Expo Centre & Mart, Expressway, Greater Noida, NCR, Delhi from 15th to 18th October 2013.

Dates: 28 & 29 November | 2013 – India IT Show (INDIASOFT) | Mumbai
Electronics and Computer Software Export Promotion Council (ESC) organized the INDIA IT SHOW (INDIASOFT) in its series of its annual INDIASOFT (IT event) during 28th & 29th November 2013 at Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

Dates: 8 – 12 January, 2014 | Reverse Buyer-Seller Meet | Bangalore
IEEMA organized Change Xchange 2014 – 2nd Reverse Buyer-Seller Meet (RBSM) concurrently with ELECRAMA-2014, the premier showcase of Indian Electrical Sector and world's largest confluence of the power transmission and distribution community. The RBSM was supported by the Department of Commerce, Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India, and was held in Bangalore, India from 8-12 January 2014. More details about IEEMA can be accessed from its website www.ieema.org

Date: 12-14 March 2014 | India International Hand-woven Fair
Handloom Export Promotion Council (HEPC) organized the fourth edition of “India International Hand-woven Fair” (IIHF-2014), an exclusive international fair for Hand-woven products, from 12th to 14th March 2014 in Chennai, India. .
