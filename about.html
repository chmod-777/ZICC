About ZICC

Zimbabwe and India have shared traditional business, cultural and social relationships. Zimbabwe India Chamber of Commerce (ZICC) emphasises to leverage this existing historical relationship for more economic tie-ups, enhanced bi-lateral trade and investments.

ZICC is a non-profit, non-political organization with a membership base comprising of various businesses, business professionals and trade experts. ZICC enjoys institutional support and ongoing relationship with the Embassy of India in Zimbabwe and the Ministry of Industry & Commerce, Government of Zimbabwe.

The Chamber not only seeks to explore various mutual business opportunities for the mutual benefit of the Indian and Zimbabwean business communities but it also aims at fostering partnership with other chambers of commerce and business associations for enhanced trade relations.

Objectives
  • Promote Zimbabwean and Indian commercial and business interests in Zimbabwe, and encourage economic and commercial activities, investments and other initiatives between India and Zimbabwe.
  • Offer its members a platform to exchange information and experiences and to communicate and interact with Public and Private entities and persons in Zimbabwe by means of meetings, gatherings, seminars, conferences, receptions.
  • Encourage mutual awareness, exchange of information and cultural cooperation between India and Zimbabwe.
  • Contribute to the development of dual and mutual economic, commercial and technical relationships between India and Zimbabwe.
  • Organize events to reinforce and improve the image of India in Zimbabwe, and Zimbabwe in India.
